# Peppermint Mocha-ish

This is a not a *true* peppermint mocha, because it's just regular drip coffee and not espresso. And it's not $6.

## Ingredients

|    Amount | Ingredient                                                                                       |
|----------:|:-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|     1 cup | Milk                                                                                             |
|     1 cup | Coffee                                                                                           |
|  1 ½ tbsp | Cocoa Powder                                                                                     |
|    2 tbsp | Sugar                                                                                            |
| 1-2 pumps | [Starbucks Peppermint Syrup](http://www.amazon.com/Starbucks-Peppermint-Syrup-1-L/dp/B004U7QTHM) |

## Directions

Obviously, brew some coffee.

In a shallow bowl, heat up the **milk** for **1 minute** in the microwave, then [froth it](http://www.amazon.com/Black-Handheld-Battery-Powered-Milk-Frother/dp/B00D0DOZLY/).

Add the **cocoa powder** and **sugar** and whisk it all together. Add the coffee and keep stirring until everything is mixed.

Add a pump or two of **peppermint syrup** and enjoy!
