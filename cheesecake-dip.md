# Cheesecake Dip

## Ingredients

|    Amount | Ingredient              |
|----------:|:------------------------|
|      8 oz | Cream Cheese (softened) |
|     ½ cup | Butter (softened)       |
|     1 tsp | Vanilla                 |
|     ¾ cup | Powdered Sugar          |
|    2 tbsp | Brown Sugar             |
|     ¾ cup | Mini Chocolate Chips    |
|     ¾ cup | Finely Chopped Walnuts  |

## Directions

Mix it all together and serve with **Chocolate Teddy Grahams**.
